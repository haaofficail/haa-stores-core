# G3 — E-Commerce License Checklist

> **Owner action required.** G3 of TASK-0038. **Depends on G1 (CR)**.

## What is the E-Commerce License?

The **رخصة التجارة الإلكترونية** (E-Commerce License) authorizes your company to conduct online sales in KSA. Issued by MoCI after CR is established.

## Prerequisites

- [ ] **G1 CR received**
- [ ] **Operating from KSA** (physical presence or registered agent)
- [ ] **Website/platform operational** (Haa is ready)
- [ ] **Privacy Policy published** (TASK-0042 §2.4 — pending owner review)
- [ ] **Terms of Service published** (TASK-0042 §8.5 — pending owner review)
- [ ] **Return/refund policy published**
- [ ] **Customer support channel** (email + phone)

## Application process (5-10 days)

### Step 1: Prepare platform documentation

- [ ] Privacy Policy URL (e.g., `haastores.sa/privacy`)
- [ ] Terms of Service URL (`haastores.sa/terms`)
- [ ] Refund policy URL (`haastores.sa/refunds`)
- [ ] Support email + phone (e.g., `support@haastores.sa` +966 11 XXX XXXX)
- [ ] Business address (matches CR)
- [ ] Sample product listings (3-5 SKUs)

### Step 2: Submit application on Maroof (1-3 days)

1. Log in to Maroof (https://maroof.sa/)
2. Navigate to "Issue E-Commerce License" (إصدار رخصة تجارة إلكترونية)
3. Fill in:
   - CR number
   - Platform URL
   - Product categories (must match CR ISIC codes)
   - Pricing model (commission / subscription / both)
4. Upload URLs of policies + screenshots
5. Pay fee (~SAR 1,000-2,000/year)
6. Submit

### Step 3: MoCI review + approval (3-7 days)

- MoCI reviews your platform + policies
- May request changes (response time: 24-48h)
- Approval = license issued (PDF + number)

### Step 4: Display the license

- Display the license number on your platform footer
- Keep the PDF accessible for inspection

## Cost

- **License fee:** SAR 1,000-2,000/year
- **Renewal:** annually
